AI Parameters Information

Here are the details of the AI parameters and functionalities used in our feedback loop:

  • Sentiment Analysis: Analyzes the polarity of the feedback text to determine the overall sentiment (positive, neutral, or negative).
  • Keyword Analysis: Identifies and counts the most frequently used words in the feedback to highlight common themes and concerns.
  • Feedback Categorization: Classifies feedback into predefined categories to better understand user satisfaction levels in different areas.
  • Pattern Identification: Tracks user interactions and behaviors to identify patterns, pain points, and areas for improvement.
  • Continuous Improvement: Regularly analyzes feedback to update AI models and algorithms based on user input, ensuring continuous improvement of our services.
